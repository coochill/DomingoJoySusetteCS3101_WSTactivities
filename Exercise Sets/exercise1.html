Video Game Developers with their Best-Selling Games

  1. Nintendo
    1. Super Mario
      • Genre: Platformer
      • Year Developed: 1985 (Super Mario Bros.)
      • Net Worth: Estimated over $22 billion (franchise)
      • One of the best-selling video game franchises of all time, featuring the adventures of Mario, a plumber in the Mushroom Kingdom.
    2. Pokémon
      • Genre: Role-Playing
      • Year Developed: 1996
      • Net Worth: Estimated over $100 billion (franchise)
      • A global phenomenon where players catch, train, and battle creatures called Pokémon.
  2. Rockstar Games
    1. Grand Theft Auto
      • Genre: Action-Adventure, Open World
      • Year Developed: 1997 (GTA)
      • Net Worth: Estimated over $9 billion (franchise)
      • An open-world series that allows players to explore cities, complete missions, and engage in various activities.
    2. Red Dead Redemption
      • Genre: Action-Adventure
      • Year Developed: 2010
      • Net Worth: Estimated over $2 billion (franchise)
      • A Western-themed action-adventure series set in the late 19th and early 20th centuries.
  3. Sony Interactive Entertainment
    1. Uncharted
      • Genre: Action-Adventure
      • Year Developed: 2007
      • Net Worth: Estimated over $1.4 billion (franchise)
      • An action-adventure series that follows the treasure-hunting adventures of Nathan Drake.
    2. The Last of Us
      • Genre: Action-Adventure, Survival Horror
      • Year Developed: 2013
      • Net Worth: Estimated over $500 million (franchise)
      • A critically acclaimed series set in a post-apocalyptic world, focusing on the emotional journey of its characters.
